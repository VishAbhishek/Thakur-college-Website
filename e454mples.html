    • WELCOME TO THAKUR POLYTECHNIC
      • Thakur Polytechnic is affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education and conducts courses approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and recognized by Govt. of Maharashtra. It fulfils all the norms and standards set by the AICTE as well as the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education.The Polytechnic was accorded approval by AICTE vide their letter no. F-740-80-819 - (F)/FT/98 dt. 24th July 1998. Thakur Polytechnic was established in the year 1998 primarily to cater to the need of students desirous of pursuing technical education after the Secondary School Certificate examination. It provides the best infrastructure facilities matched by equally competent teaching faculty & a right academic ambience to make learning a delight full experience.
